What fi.e does

Every file. Always findable.

fi.e wraps a semantic AI layer around your existing storage — no migration, no new habits, just instant intelligence.

Semantic search

Ask in plain language — "the budget we sent to Acme in March" — and fi.e finds it even if those words aren't in the filename.

Auto-organization

fi.e reads your files and auto-generates smart collections — contracts, reports, media — without you lifting a finger.

Content awareness

fi.e doesn't just index filenames. It reads PDFs, understands spreadsheets, and extracts key data from every document.

Team context

fi.e learns your team's patterns. It surfaces files relevant to ongoing projects and flags stale or conflicting versions automatically.

Any storage, one view

Connect Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and local storage. fi.e creates a single, unified, AI-indexed view of everything.

Privacy-first

All indexing happens locally or in your own cloud. We never train on your files, ever. Your data is yours, always.
